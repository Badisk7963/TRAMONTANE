# Bolt.new — Homepage Analysis
> Source: https://bolt.new (scraped 2026-03-30)

## Positioning
- **Tagline:** "What will you build today?"
- **Hero:** "Create stunning apps & websites by chatting with AI."
- **Meta:** "Build and scale high-performing websites & apps using your words. Join millions and start building today."
- **OG Title:** "Bolt AI builder: Websites, apps & prototypes"
- **Parent company:** StackBlitz

## Key Value Props
1. **"Always the best, without switching tools"** — integrates frontier coding agents from AI labs
2. **"98% less errors"** — auto tests, refactors, iterates
3. **"Build big without breaking"** — handles projects 1,000x larger than before
4. **"Build with your design system"** — stop building from scratch, build on-brand

## Entry Points
- **Plan** button (planning mode before build)
- **Build now** button (direct generation)
- Import from **Figma** or **GitHub**

## Built-in Features (Homepage Claims)
- Unlimited databases
- Enterprise-grade infrastructure
- User management & authentication
- SEO optimization
- Hosting with analytics & custom domains
- Design system support

## Target Personas
- Product managers: "insight to prototype in hours"
- Entrepreneurs: "launch a full business in days"
- Marketers: "high-performing campaign pages in hours"
- Agencies: "multiply your impact, deliver more projects"
- Students & builders: "learn by doing"

## UX Observation
- Plan/Build toggle prominent in main CTA
- Figma and GitHub import right on homepage
- No social proof numbers visible on homepage (unlike Lovable's 36M)
- "The #1 professional vibe coding tool" claim without specific numbers
