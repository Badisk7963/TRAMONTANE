# Lovable — Pricing Analysis
> Source: https://lovable.dev/pricing (scraped 2026-03-30)

## Pricing Tiers

### Free — $0/month (EUR 0)
- No credit card needed, free forever
- 5 daily credits (up to 30/month)
- Public projects only
- Unlimited collaborators
- 5 lovable.app domains
- Cloud access

### Pro — $25/month (EUR ~23) shared across unlimited users
- 100 monthly credits
- 5 daily credits (up to 150/month total)
- Usage-based Cloud + AI
- Credit rollovers
- On-demand credit top-ups
- Unlimited lovable.app domains
- Custom domains
- Remove Lovable badge
- User roles & permissions

### Business — $50/month (EUR ~46) shared across unlimited users
- 100 monthly credits
- All Pro features plus:
- Internal publish
- SSO
- Team workspace
- Personal projects
- Design templates
- Role-based access
- Security center

### Enterprise — Platform fee (based on company size, covering all employees)
- All Business features plus:
- Volume-based credit pricing
- Dedicated support
- Onboarding services
- Design systems
- SCIM
- Support for custom connectors
- Publishing controls
- Sharing controls
- Audit logs

## Credit System
- Credits are the usage unit (not tokens)
- 1 credit = 1 Plan mode message
- Agent mode messages likely cost more credits
- Daily free credits accrue even on paid plans
- Credit rollovers available on Pro+
- On-demand top-ups available

## Additional Offerings
- **Gift cards** — can send to friends
- **Student discount** — up to 50% off Pro (with verification)
- **$25 Cloud + $1 AI** free monthly per workspace until end of Q1 2026

## Key Observations for ArkhosAI
- Pricing is PER WORKSPACE, not per user — very competitive for teams
- $25/mo for unlimited users is aggressive pricing
- Credit system is opaque — "what is a credit" is in FAQ but not clear on page
- No transparent per-token pricing visible (unlike v0)
- Student discount = growth play for adoption
