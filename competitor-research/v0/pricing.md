# v0 by Vercel — Pricing Analysis
> Sources: v0.dev/pricing, vercel.com/blog/updated-v0-pricing, vercel.com/pricing (scraped 2026-03-30)

## v0 Plans

| Plan | Price | Credits | Key Features |
|------|-------|---------|--------------|
| Free | $0/mo (EUR 0) | $5/mo | 7 msg/day, Vercel deploy, Design Mode, GitHub sync |
| Team | $30/user/mo (EUR ~27.60) | $30/user/mo + $2 daily | Shared credits, team collaboration, centralized billing |
| Business | $100/user/mo (EUR ~92) | $30/user/mo + $2 daily | Training opt-out, all Team features |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | No training, SAML SSO, RBAC, priority access, SLAs |

## Vercel Hosting (Additional Cost)
- Hobby: Free
- Pro: $20/mo (EUR ~18.40) + usage
- Enterprise: Custom

## Total Cost for Team User
- v0 Team: $30/user/mo
- Vercel Pro: $20/mo
- **Total: ~$50/user/mo (EUR ~46)** minimum before usage overages

## Key Pricing Insights
- Token-based since May 2025 (moved from message counts)
- Purchased credits expire after 1 year
- Training opt-out requires Business ($100/user) or Enterprise
- Most transparent pricing of all three competitors
- Per-user pricing makes it most expensive for teams
